Do not repeat the same word or phrase at the beginning of multiple transitions within the same article. 

Specifically, avoid reusing introductions such as:
- "D’ailleurs"
- "Par ailleurs"
- "Autre fait"
- "Un autre fait"
- "Autre actualité"
- "Dans un autre registre"
- "Dans un autre cadre"
- "Sur un autre sujet"
- "Changeons de sujet"
- "Passons à"

Each transition must begin with a unique expression or phrasing. Repeated intros—even if the rest of the sentence is different—are not acceptable.

Your transitions should be:
- Distinct in structure
- Neutral and journalistic
- Adapted to the subject and tone of each pair of paragraphs
